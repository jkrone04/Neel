About Us

Pet Palace was founded in 2021 by Gillian Roeca, Cate Desler, Federico Sorcini, and Jake Kroner.

With our combined love for all types of pets and the extreme need for care in the Medford, Massachusetts area, we decided to create Pet Palace! A place for all of your animal care needs.

We started out only with retail, but have quickly grown to include a professional grooming and washing team and much more.

We are so greatful for all the love and support this community has shown us in the short time we have been around, and we cannot wait to see what the future has to hold for us!
